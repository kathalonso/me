About Me

The professional and personal chronicles of a Kathalina.


My story

Raised in a Spanish-English bilingual household, my tongue was the sharpest tool I had and I wanted to refine it even more by understanding language as a science. From there, I studied linguistics and areas complementing it. Briefly, I was a special education teacher assistant and found significant value in shadowing speech-language pathologists. Drawing upon their techniques enhanced my experience as an English teacher allowing me to cultivate students' fluency and accent reduction.

But everything changed when the fire nation attacked ... Just kidding! I went on to earn my master's in la bellisima Barcelona.
